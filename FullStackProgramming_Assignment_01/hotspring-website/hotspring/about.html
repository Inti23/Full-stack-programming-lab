About Us

Welcome to the Company

This is HotSpring Portable Spas — your premier destination for luxury hot tubs and portable spa solutions. For over two decades, we have been providing customers with the finest quality spas at unbeatable prices. Our commitment to excellence begins with hand-selecting each product from the world's most trusted spa manufacturers.

We believe that everyone deserves to experience the therapeutic benefits of hydrotherapy. That's why we carry an extensive selection of portable spas, from intimate 2-person models to expansive 8+ person entertainment hubs. Each product in our catalog meets rigorous quality standards and comes backed by comprehensive warranty coverage.

Our expert team is available 24/7 to guide you through every step of your purchase — from selecting the perfect model to ensuring seamless installation. With free nationwide shipping and a low price guarantee, shopping for your dream spa has never been easier.
